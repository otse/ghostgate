nnpcs - non-non-playing characters

the word nnpc is a bit tongue in cheek; maybe fnpc for "free npc" would be better

nnpcs are basically player characters

nnpcs are not "crazy npcs"

nnpcs have a backstory, a mission or itinerary, and do most things an npc wouldn't

nnpcs explore more, fight more, loot and buy more than regular npcs

(example)
thaduun the learner - chaser of the school of destruction

his backstory is that he is a curious dark elf
his mission is a pilgrimage to shrines and temples
he could have a unique fire pendant that glows when he approaches ancient places
